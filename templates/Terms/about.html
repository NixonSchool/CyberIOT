Welcome to CyberIOT Forum

CyberIOT Forum is the ultimate online community for IoT enthusiasts, cybersecurity professionals, and tech innovators. Our platform brings together a diverse group of individuals passionate about the Internet of Things and its secure implementation.

Our Mission

To foster a collaborative environment where IoT enthusiasts can learn, share, and innovate while prioritizing cybersecurity in the rapidly evolving world of connected devices.

What We Offer

  • Forum: Engage in discussions, ask questions, and share your knowledge with a community of like-minded individuals.
  • Marketplace: Buy, sell, or trade IoT devices, components, and related services in our secure marketplace.
  • Vulnerability Reporting: Contribute to the security of IoT ecosystems by reporting vulnerabilities you discover.
  • Vulnerability Tracking: Stay informed about the latest IoT security issues that have been fixed and contribute a little by writing articles.
  • Chat: Connect with other members in real-time through our integrated chat system.
  • Learning Hub: Access a wealth of educational content, including tutorials, webinars, and courses, in our YouTube-like learning platform.

Our Community

CyberIOT Forum is home to a diverse group of professionals, hobbyists, and learners, including:

  • IoT Developers and Engineers
  • Cybersecurity Experts
  • Hardware Enthusiasts
  • Data Scientists and Analysts
  • Product Managers and Entrepreneurs
  • Students and Educators
